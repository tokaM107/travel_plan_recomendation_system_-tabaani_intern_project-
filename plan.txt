Okay, here's a detailed 6-day luxury family itinerary for Morocco, focusing on Casablanca and Marrakech, balancing historical exploration with adventurous activities, all while maintaining a high level of comfort and service.

**Trip Overview:**

*   **Focus:** Cultural immersion, historical sites, adventure, luxury experiences.
*   **Travel Style:** Private guided tours, luxury accommodations, comfortable transportation.
*   **Family Considerations:** Activities are designed to be engaging for a family with children (adaptable based on ages).
*   **Budget:** High-end (expect significant costs for private guides, luxury riads/hotels, and bespoke experiences).

**Day 1: Arrival in Casablanca & Coastal Charm**

*   **Morning (9:00 AM):**
    *   **Arrival at Casablanca's Mohammed V International Airport (CMN).**
    *   **Private Transfer:** Be greeted by a private driver and transferred to your luxury hotel. (Sofitel Casablanca Tour Blanche, Four Seasons Hotel Casablanca)
    *   **Check-in and Refresh:** Settle into your accommodations and take some time to relax.
*   **Afternoon (12:00 PM):**
    *   **Lunch at Rick's Café:** Enjoy a memorable lunch at the iconic Rick's Café, inspired by the film "Casablanca." (Reservations are essential).
*   **Afternoon (2:00 PM):**
    *   **Hassan II Mosque Tour:** A guided tour of the magnificent Hassan II Mosque, one of the largest mosques in the world.  Its stunning architecture and oceanfront location are breathtaking.  Book a private guide for a more personalized experience.
*   **Evening (6:00 PM):**
    *   **Corniche Stroll:** Take a leisurely walk along the Corniche (coastal promenade), enjoying the ocean breeze and the vibrant atmosphere.
*   **Evening (8:00 PM):**
    *   **Dinner at Le Cabestan - Ocean View:** Enjoy a fine-dining experience with stunning ocean views at Le Cabestan.  They specialize in seafood and Moroccan cuisine.

**Day 2: Casablanca History & Marrakech Arrival**

*   **Morning (9:00 AM):**
    *   **Jewish Heritage Tour:** Explore Casablanca's Jewish heritage with a visit to the Museum of Moroccan Judaism, one of the only museums dedicated to Jewish history in the Arab world. Also visit the Beth-El Temple, the largest temple in Casablanca.
*   **Morning (11:00 AM):**
    *   **Old Medina Exploration:** Wander through the charming, less-touristed Old Medina of Casablanca.  It offers a more authentic glimpse into local life.
*   **Afternoon (1:00 PM):**
    *   **Lunch at a Local Restaurant:** Enjoy a traditional Moroccan lunch at a restaurant in the Medina (e.g., *Restaurant Al Mounia* for a mix of Moroccan and French cuisine).
*   **Afternoon (3:00 PM):**
    *   **Private Transfer to Marrakech:** Enjoy a comfortable private transfer to Marrakech (approximately 3-4 hours).  Your driver will ensure a smooth and scenic journey.
*   **Evening (6:00 PM):**
    *   **Check-in to Luxury Riad:** Arrive in Marrakech and check into your luxury riad in the Medina (e.g., Royal Mansour Marrakech, La Mamounia, Riad Kniza). A riad offers a more intimate and authentic Moroccan experience.
*   **Evening (8:00 PM):**
    *   **Riad Dinner:** Enjoy a delicious welcome dinner at your riad.  Many riads offer exceptional culinary experiences.

**Day 3: Marrakech Medina & Majorelle Garden**

*   **Morning (9:00 AM):**
    *   **Guided Medina Tour:** Embark on a private guided tour of the Marrakech Medina, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.  Explore the souks (markets), learn about the history of the city, and visit key landmarks.
*   **Morning (11:00 AM):**
    *   **Bahia Palace:** Visit the Bahia Palace, a stunning example of Moroccan architecture, showcasing intricate details and beautiful gardens.
*   **Afternoon (1:00 PM):**
    *   **Lunch at Nomad:** Enjoy a modern Moroccan lunch with rooftop views at Nomad in the Medina.
*   **Afternoon (3:00 PM):**
    *   **Majorelle Garden & Yves Saint Laurent Museum:** Explore the vibrant Majorelle Garden, a botanical garden with striking blue architecture.  Afterward, visit the adjacent Yves Saint Laurent Museum, dedicated to the iconic designer's connection to Marrakech.
*   **Evening (6:00 PM):**
    *   **Hammam Experience:** Indulge in a traditional Moroccan hammam experience at a luxury spa (e.g., at your riad or a dedicated spa like Les Bains de Marrakech).
*   **Evening (8:00 PM):**
    *   **Dinner at Le Foundouk:** Enjoy a dinner at Le Foundouk, a restaurant set in a beautifully restored riad, offering Moroccan cuisine with a modern twist.

**Day 4: Atlas Mountains Adventure**

*   **Morning (9:00 AM):**
    *   **Atlas Mountains Day Trip:** Embark on a private day trip to the Atlas Mountains.  Your driver and guide will take you to scenic valleys, Berber villages, and stunning viewpoints.
*   **Morning (10:30 AM):**
    *   **Camel Ride:** Enjoy a short camel ride through the Agafay Desert or in the foothills of the Atlas Mountains for a memorable experience.
*   **Afternoon (1:00 PM):**
    *   **Lunch in a Berber Village:** Have lunch at a traditional Berber home or a restaurant with panoramic mountain views.  Enjoy authentic Moroccan cuisine.
*   **Afternoon (3:00 PM):**
    *   **Hiking or Trekking:** Opt for a guided hike or trek in the Atlas Mountains, tailored to your family's fitness level.  Explore the stunning landscapes and interact with local Berber communities.
*   **Evening (6:00 PM):**
    *   **Return to Marrakech:** Return to Marrakech in the late afternoon.
*   **Evening (8:00 PM):**
    *   **Dinner at Comptoir Darna:** Enjoy a dinner at Comptoir Darna, a restaurant with a lively atmosphere, belly dancing performances, and delicious Moroccan cuisine.

**Day 5: Hot Air Balloon & Cooking Class**

*   **Morning (6:00 AM):**
    *   **Hot Air Balloon Ride:** Experience a breathtaking hot air balloon ride over the Marrakech countryside at sunrise.  Enjoy stunning views of the Atlas Mountains and the surrounding landscape. (Book in advance).
*   **Morning (9:00 AM):**
    *   **Breakfast:** Enjoy a late breakfast back at your riad.
*   **Afternoon (11:00 AM):**
    *   **Moroccan Cooking Class:** Participate in a hands-on Moroccan cooking class.  Learn to prepare traditional dishes like tagine, couscous, and Moroccan salads. (Many riads or cooking schools offer classes).
*   **Afternoon (2:00 PM):**
    *   **Lunch (Your Creations):** Enjoy the delicious meal you prepared during your cooking class.
*   **Afternoon (4:00 PM):**
    *   **Souk Shopping:** Spend the afternoon exploring the souks of Marrakech.  Bargain for souvenirs, spices, leather goods, and other treasures.
*   **Evening (7:00 PM):**
    *   **Jemaa el-Fna Square:** Visit Jemaa el-Fna Square in the evening.  Experience the vibrant atmosphere with storytellers, musicians, food stalls, and snake charmers.
*   **Evening (8:30 PM):**
    *   **Dinner at Terrace des Epices:** Enjoy a casual dinner at Terrace des Epices, a rooftop restaurant in the Medina with stunning views of Jemaa el-Fna Square.

**Day 6: Departure**

*   **Morning (9:00 AM):**
    *   **Relax and Enjoy:** Enjoy a leisurely breakfast and relax at your riad.  Perhaps get a final massage or enjoy the pool.
*   **Morning (11:00 AM):**
    *   **Last-Minute Souvenir Shopping:** If desired, do some last-minute souvenir shopping.
*   **Afternoon (1:00 PM):**
    *   **Lunch:** Enjoy a final Moroccan lunch at a restaurant of your choice.
*   **Afternoon (3:00 PM):**
    *   **Private Transfer to Marrakech Menara Airport (RAK):** Be transferred to Marrakech Menara Airport for your departure.

**Luxury Considerations:**

*   **Accommodations:**
    *   **Casablanca:** Sofitel Casablanca Tour Blanche, Four Seasons Hotel Casablanca
    *   **Marrakech:** Royal Mansour Marrakech, La Mamounia, Riad Kniza, Amanjena (outside of the Medina for a more resort-like experience)
*   **Transportation:** Private drivers with comfortable, air-conditioned vehicles.  Consider a luxury SUV for the Atlas Mountains trip.
*   **Guides:**  Licensed, knowledgeable, and English-speaking private guides for all tours and excursions.
*   **Experiences:**
    *   Private cooking classes.
    *   Exclusive access to sites and attractions.
    *   Personalized service at riads and hotels.
    *   Spa treatments at luxury spas.
*   **Dining:**  Reservations at top-rated restaurants.  Consider private dining experiences at your riad.

**Family Adaptations:**

*   **Children's Activities:**  Consider including activities specifically for children, such as a visit to the Jardin Secret in Marrakech, a camel ride designed for younger children, or a pottery workshop.
*   **Pace:**  Adjust the pace of the itinerary to suit your family's needs.  Allow for downtime and rest periods.
*   **Food:**  Be mindful of your children's dietary preferences and allergies.  Many restaurants offer kid-friendly options.
*   **Accommodation:** Book family suites or connecting rooms at your chosen accommodations.

**Important Notes:**

*   **Booking:** Book flights, accommodations, tours, and activities well in advance, especially during peak season.
*   **Visas:** Check visa requirements for your nationality.
*   **Currency:** The currency in Morocco is the Moroccan Dirham (MAD).  Credit cards are widely accepted in larger establishments, but it's a good idea to have cash for smaller shops and souks.
*   **Dress Code:** Dress modestly, especially when visiting religious sites.  Women should cover their shoulders and knees.
*   **Bargaining:** Bargaining is expected in the souks.  Be polite and friendly, and don't be afraid to negotiate.
*   **Health:** Consult your doctor about necessary vaccinations and health precautions.  Drink bottled water and avoid ice.
*   **Safety:** Morocco is generally a safe country, but it's always wise to be aware of your surroundings and take precautions against petty theft.

This itinerary is a starting point and can be customized to your family's specific interests and preferences.  Enjoy your luxurious and unforgettable adventure in Morocco!
